Privacy Policy

Last Updated: October 18, 2025

Important: This application is used solely for managing Facebook and Instagram advertising campaigns. We do not collect, store, or share any personal user data beyond what is necessary for ad campaign management.

1. Information We Access

Our application accesses the following information through the Facebook Marketing API:

  • Ad Account Information: Account ID, currency, and campaign data
  • Campaign Data: Ad campaigns, ad sets, and ad creatives created through our tool
  • Page Information: Basic page details (name, ID) for ad creation purposes
  • Product Data: Information from your Shopify store (if connected) for ad creation

2. How We Use This Information

The information accessed is used exclusively to:

  • Create and manage advertising campaigns on Facebook and Instagram
  • Generate ad copy and creative assets
  • Provide budget and targeting recommendations
  • Display campaign performance data within our application

3. Data Storage and Security

We prioritize your data security:

  • Local Storage: All credentials (API tokens, access keys) are stored locally on your device in encrypted environment files (.env)
  • No Cloud Storage: We do not store your Facebook credentials or campaign data on any external servers
  • Session-Based: Data is only accessed during active sessions when you use the application
  • No Third-Party Sharing: We never share, sell, or transfer your data to third parties

4. Facebook Data Usage

Our application complies with Facebook Platform Policies:

  • We only request the minimum permissions necessary for ad management
  • We do not access user profiles, friends lists, or personal Facebook data
  • We do not post to Facebook or Instagram on your behalf
  • All ad campaigns are created in PAUSED status for your review before activation

5. Shopify Integration (Optional)

If you connect your Shopify store:

  • We access product information (titles, descriptions, prices, images)
  • This data is used solely for creating ad content
  • No customer data or order information is accessed
  • Product data is not stored permanently

6. Your Rights and Control

You have complete control over your data:

  • Revoke Access: You can revoke app permissions at any time through Facebook Business Settings
  • Delete Data: Simply delete your local .env file to remove all stored credentials
  • Transparency: All API calls made by the application are logged in the console for your review

7. Cookies and Tracking

This application:

  • Does not use cookies
  • Does not track your browsing activity
  • Does not use analytics or tracking pixels

8. Third-Party Services

We integrate with the following services:

  • Facebook Marketing API: For ad campaign management - Facebook Privacy Policy
  • Shopify API: For product data (if connected) - Shopify Privacy Policy
  • Google Trends (Optional): For keyword research - Google Privacy Policy

9. Data Retention

  • No Data Retention: We do not retain any data beyond your active session
  • Temporary Processing: Data is processed in memory and discarded when you close the application
  • Local Credentials: Only you control credential storage via your .env file

10. Children's Privacy

This application is intended for business use only and is not directed at individuals under 18 years of age. We do not knowingly collect information from minors.

11. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected in the "Last Updated" date at the top of this page.

12. Contact Information

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy or our data practices, please contact us:

  • Email: privacy@yourcompany.com
  • Method: Through your Facebook Business Manager support

13. Legal Compliance

This application complies with:

  • Facebook Platform Terms and Policies
  • General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR)
  • California Consumer Privacy Act (CCPA)
  • Applicable e-commerce and advertising regulations
